Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rewards program

How do I earn points?

You earn points by recycling your e-waste at our certified drop-off locations. The number of points depends on the type and quantity of items recycled.

How long do my points last?

Your points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned. We'll send you reminders before any points are due to expire.

How quickly will I receive my reward after redeeming?

Most digital rewards like airtime and data bundles are delivered instantly. Physical rewards or vouchers may take 3-5 business days to arrive.

Can I transfer my points to someone else?

Currently, points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder who earned them.
